About AudioHD Pro

AudioHD Pro is a powerful browser extension that enhances your audio experience with advanced features like a customizable equalizer, dynamic range compressor, reverb effects, and 3D spatial audio. Whether you're listening to music, watching movies, or playing games, AudioHD Pro gives you full control over your sound, allowing you to tailor it to your preferences. With site-specific profiles and keyboard shortcuts, you can easily switch settings on the fly for different websites. Experience your audio like never before with AudioHD Pro!
